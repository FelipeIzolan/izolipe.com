## Vowels & Consonants

Vowels and consonants are types of speech sounds, based on how we make the sound of each letter.

---

### Vowels

<Image src={Image2} alt="English Vowels"/>
> **A, E, I, O, U**

Vowels are the sounds we make when we speak without blocking the air with our mouth.

#### The 'y' letter

<Image src={Image3} alt="The 'Y' vowel"/>
> **Y**

The letter 'y' can be a vowel or a consonant depending on the word.

##### Words with 'Y' as a Vowel

- **Fly**
- **Myth**
- **Reply**

##### Words with 'Y' as a Consonant

- **You**
- **Young**
- **Yogurt**

---

### Consonants

<Image src={Image4} alt="English Consonants"/>
> **B, C, D, F, G, H, J, K, L, M, N, P, Q, R, S, T, V, W, X, Y, Z**

Consonants are the sounds we make when we block or restrict the flow of air with our mouth.
