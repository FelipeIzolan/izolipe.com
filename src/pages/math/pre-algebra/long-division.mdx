Long division is a method for dividing large numbers digit-by-digit.

**Divisor**: The number that divides.\
**Dividend**: The number that is to be divided.

---

## Long Division Steps

<Image src={Image1} alt='Long division example'/>

1. **Setup**: Write the dividend inside the division box, and the divisor outside the box.
2. **Divide**: Look at the digit of the dividend. Determine how many times the divisor fits into it, and write the answer above the division box.
3. **Multiply**: Multiply the divisor by the number you wrote above the division box.
4. **Subtract**: Subtract the result from the digit or digits you are working with. Write the remainder below.
5. **Bring down**: Bring down the next digit of the dividend next to the remainder.
6. **Repeat**: Repeat the process until youâ€™ve used all the digits in the dividend.
