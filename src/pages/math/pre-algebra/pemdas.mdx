**Order of Operations (PEMDAS)** is the rule for the **correct sequence to evaluate a math expression**.
> **1** - **P**arenthesis\
> **2** - **E**xponent\
> **3** - **M**ultiplication\
> **4** - **D**ivision\
> **5** - **A**ddition\
> **6** - **S**ubtraction

---

## Examples

$
10+7*2\\
10+14\\
24
$


$
32 \div (2+2)^2\\
32 \div 4^2\\
32 \div 16\\
2
$


$
80 + 10 \div 5 * 2\\
80 + 2 * 2\\
80 + 4\\
84
$
