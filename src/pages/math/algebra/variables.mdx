Variable is a **symbol** that represents a **mathematical object**.

---

Consider the expression: $2x + 1$

<table>
  <thead>
    <th>x</th>
    <th>Expression</th>
    <th>Result</th>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>3</td>
      <td>$2 * 3 + 1$</td>
      <td>$7$</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>$\sqrt[3]{64}$</td>
      <td>$2 * \sqrt[3]{64} + 1$</td>
      <td>$9$</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>$\log_4(16384)$</td>
      <td>$2 * \log_4(16384) + 1$</td>
      <td>$15$</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

> You can **replace the variable x** with **any mathematical object** and then calculate the result.

---
