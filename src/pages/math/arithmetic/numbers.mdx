## Types of Numbers

<a href='https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Venn_Diagram_of_Numbers_Expanded.svg'>
<Image loading='lazy' src={Image1} alt="Venn Diagram of Numbers" />
</a>

- **Natural($\N$)**: All the whole numbers from one to infinity.
- **Integer($\Z$)**: All the whole numbers that can be positive, negative or zero.
- **Rational($\mathbb{Q}$)**: All the numbers that are a ratio of two integers.
- **Irrational($\mathbb{I}$)**: All the numbers that aren't a rational number.
- **Real($\mathbb{R}$)**: All the numbers that can be represented on the number line.

### Examples
$\N = \set{1, 2, 6, 24, 128, ...}$\
$\Z = \set{-10, -4, -1, 0, 1, 4, 10, ...}$\
$\mathbb{Q} = \set{-10/2, -5, \sqrt{81}, 9, 10.50/2, 5.25, ...}$\
$\mathbb{I} = \set{\sqrt{2}, \sqrt{8}, \pi, e, ...}$\
$\mathbb{R} = \set{3.5, -24/8, \pi, \sqrt{2}, 54, -33, 2/8, ...}$
