## For every real number $a$, has a negative $-a$

$a + (-a) = 0$

### Examples:

- $77$ - $(-77)$
- $12.\bar{6}$ - $(-12.\bar{6})$
- $1.41421356237$ - $(-1.41421356237)$

## Properties of Negative

To works with negative numbers, we use following properties.

<table>
  <tr>
    <th>Index</th>
    <th>Property</th>
    <th>Example</th>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td style='text-align:center'>**1**</td>
    <td>$(-1)x = -x$</td>
    <td>$(-1)5 = -5$</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td style='text-align:center'>**2**</td>

    <td>$-(-5) = 5$</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td style='text-align:center'>**3**</td>
    <td>$(-x)y = x(-y) = -(xy)$</td>
    <td>$(-2)3 = 2(-3) = -(2*3)$</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td style='text-align:center'>**4**</td>
    <td>$(-x)(-y) = xy$</td>
    <td>$(-10)(-7) = 10 * 7$</td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td style='text-align:center'>**5**</td>
    <td>$-(x + y) = -x - y$</td>

  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td style='text-align:center'>**6**</td>
    <td>$-(x - y) = y - x$</td>
    <td>$-(3 - 5) = 5 - 3$</td>
  </tr>
